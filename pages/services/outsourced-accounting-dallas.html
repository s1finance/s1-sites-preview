Two Kinds of Financials

Here's something most business owners don't know: there are two kinds of financials.

Tax Financials

What your CPA prepares. Built for the IRS — organized for compliance, backward-looking by design. Important? Absolutely. But they don't help you run the business.

Management Financials

Built for you — the owner. They show where your cash goes, which customers are actually profitable, what your margins look like by service line, and whether you can afford that next hire. This is the financial intelligence that helps you make decisions.

Your CPA handles one kind. We handle the other. Both matter — they're different jobs.

Most small businesses are running on tax financials alone. That's like driving with nothing but a rearview mirror.

Common Questions

How is this different from just hiring a bookkeeper?

A bookkeeper records transactions. We do that too — but we also close your books monthly, build custom reports, analyze your profitability, project your cash flow, and flag problems before they become emergencies. You get a complete financial function, not just data entry.

Do you replace my CPA?

No. Your CPA handles taxes. We handle everything else — the management side of your financials. We actually make your CPA's job easier by keeping the books clean and organized year-round. Most of our clients keep their CPA and work with us alongside them.

What does outsourced accounting cost for a small business?

It depends on the complexity of your business — transaction volume, number of entities, reporting needs. We'll scope it in the discovery call. No surprises, no hidden fees.

Can you fix messy books?

Yes. Most owners we work with come to us with books that are behind, disorganized, or unreliable. We clean them up, build a reliable process, and then keep them running. The cleanup is usually the hardest part — after that, it's smooth.

Do I have to be in Dallas?

We're headquartered in Dallas and serve clients across Texas and beyond. All of our work is done remotely — we meet over video, use cloud-based tools, and deliver reports digitally.
