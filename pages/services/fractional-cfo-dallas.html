What a Fractional CFO Does

A fractional CFO gives you executive-level financial leadership — without the $250K+ salary of a full-time hire.

We work with you on the financial decisions that shape where your business goes next. Not bookkeeping. Not tax prep. Strategy.

Financial planning and analysis
  • Cash flow forecasting and scenario modeling
  • Annual budgets and monthly variance analysis

Strategic financial support

Operational finance

  • Vendor contract review and cost optimization
  • Team financial literacy — helping your managers understand their numbers

The Investor Approach

Most fractional CFOs come from an accounting background. S1 takes an investor's approach — we analyze your business the way a portfolio manager would, looking at cash dynamics, growth economics, risk factors, and competitive positioning.

That means your financial reporting doesn't just summarize last month. It informs next quarter.

Two Kinds of Financials

Your CPA handles the financials built for the IRS. They're essential — compliance, tax planning, filing. That work is backward-looking by design.

We handle the financials built for you. The ones that show which customers are profitable, whether you can afford your next hire, what happens to cash if a key client pays late, and where to invest next. These are the financials that help you run the business — and plan where it goes.

Your CPA handles one side. We handle the other. Both matter. They're different jobs.

Common Questions

How is a fractional CFO different from my CPA?

Your CPA handles taxes. We handle everything else — the management side of your financials. Cash planning, financial analysis, strategic support, and the reporting that helps you make decisions. Most of our clients keep their CPA and work with us alongside them.

How much time do you spend with each client?

It varies. Some clients need 10–15 hours per month, others need 20–30. We scope it based on the complexity of your business and the decisions on your plate. The engagement flexes as your needs change.

Do I need a fractional CFO, or just better accounting?

If your books are behind or unreliable, start with accounting. If your books are clean but you're not getting strategic value from your financial data, that's where a fractional CFO helps. Not sure? That's what the discovery call is for.

Can you work with my existing bookkeeper or accounting team?

Yes. We often work alongside in-house bookkeepers or accounting staff. We provide the strategic layer — the analysis, planning, and decision support — while your team handles the day-to-day operations.

Do I have to be in Dallas?

We're headquartered in Dallas and serve clients across Texas and beyond. All of our work is done remotely — we meet over video, use cloud-based tools, and deliver reports digitally.
