Common Questions

How is this different from using QuickBooks and doing it myself?

QuickBooks is a tool. It doesn't do anything on its own. Most business owners who "use QuickBooks" have messy categorizations, unreconciled accounts, and no reliable reports. We use your existing software — but we run it properly, consistently, and with processes that produce numbers you can actually trust.

Do you replace my CPA?

No. Your CPA handles taxes. We handle the rest — keeping your books clean year-round so tax time isn't a scramble. We actually make your CPA's job easier and faster (which can save you money on tax prep too).

What software do you work with?

We work with QuickBooks Online, Xero, and other major cloud-based platforms. If you're not on a platform yet, we'll help you pick the right one and get set up.

How much does bookkeeping cost for a small business?

It depends on transaction volume, number of accounts, and complexity. We scope it in the discovery call and give you a clear monthly price. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Can you fix books that are really behind?

Yes. That's actually how most of our bookkeeping relationships start. We clean up the backlog, build a reliable process, and then keep things running. The cleanup is the hardest part — after that, it's smooth.

Do I have to be in Dallas?

We're headquartered in Dallas and serve clients across Texas and beyond. All of our work is done remotely — we meet over video, use cloud-based tools, and deliver reports digitally.
